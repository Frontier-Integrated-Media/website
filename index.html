FRONTIER MEDIA

Media Production & Marketing

WHO WE ARE

With roots in Sydney, Australia, Frontier Media is an international media production and marketing company composed of talented creators from around the world.

WHAT WE DO

The counterpart of our creative department, our commercial department offers motion graphic video solutions worldwide and live video solutions within Sydney, Australia. With our proven, tight-knit team, we produce affordable, high-quality media in record time. We strive to make the process as easy for you as possible, offering custom media solutions that suit your needs. At the end of the day, we want to make you happy, and we'll work with you until you've got exactly what you want.

THE PROCESS

You may require a specific, individual service — such as scriptwriting or graphic design — or an all-inclusive video in which we take your idea and bring it to life through any combination of motion graphics, live video, narration, music, and sound effects.

Either way, the process begins with personalised correspondence, through which we'll come to see your vision just as you do. Once your idea is clear to us, we'll then produce a draft — whether that be an individual script, individual graphic, or an all-inclusive video — and share it with you so you can guide us with the finishing touches.

Part of the process is defining your budget, and to make that easier, we've created two motion graphic video examples, one on a medium budget, and another on a larger budget. This way, you can see precisely what your dollars will get you.
